The Follow-Up Problem

We Have No Idea What's Actually Working

2,068 leads generated. But how many were called? How many booked? How many became members? Without proper follow-up and tracking, we're flying blind.

The Reality

Lead gen costs look great on paper. But if leads aren't being followed up properly, we're just paying for names in a database. Sales conversion is where the real value lives.

The Solution

Every lead needs 3-5 contact attempts minimum. Track every call, every booking, every conversion. Without this data, we can't optimise for what actually drives revenue.

The Strategy

We didn't artificially inflate booking page numbers. Ads drove traffic to landing pages where people could explore first. Only when they clicked "Book Now" did they reach the actual booking system.

That extra step filtered casual browsers from serious bookers.

The Numbers

Fewer people, but...

We traded volume for intent. The visitors who reached the booking page weren't window shopping—they were actively comparing tee times and ready to book.
